Our idea

As we start learn how to code and not often have engouh money to purschase an paid course.
We mostly like to go for the free available sources, which exist a lot, but all way to much spread around all over the internet.
Our idea is to turn that around and create a place where all the new & the more experienced codes and come alog and get all that information in one place!

The undercode project is an open-sourced one, open to all developers who wish to contribute and help grow the technology ecosystem. With that said, we are an Open Source community, Creating and giving.
